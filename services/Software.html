Software Solutions

At Zeos Systems, we create software products for academia and businesses, and as such, security is a fundamental component we build into our software from the outset.

We have observed an increasing trend of applications moving to the cloud, which complicates the implementation of a zero-trust environment essential for keeping your data secure. At some point, your data is unencrypted on a server during processing, which inherently carries risks. Alongside this shift to the cloud, subscription models have become the predominant method for using software. This trend makes it increasingly difficult for users to truly own their software and data.

In response to these challenges, we at Zeos Systems have decided to take a stand. We are committed to ensuring that our users retain control and ownership of their data. By processing data locally whenever possible and employing cutting-edge homomorphic encryption when the cloud is necessary, we ensure that data remains encrypted even while being processed. This revolutionary approach not only mitigates risks but also provides peace of mind to our users.

Our dedication to security and data ownership is part of our broader mission to empower users with reliable, trustworthy, and innovative software solutions. We believe that by giving power back to the user, we are ushering in a new era of data ownership and security.

Products

Convergence

Convergence is an AI-powered chat application that enables seamless integration with Large Language Models (LLMs) without requiring an internet connection. By running locally on your computer, Convergence eliminates the need for a constant online connection, allowing you to access and utilize LLM models in real-time, regardless of your location or network status. What's more, inspired by the innovative work of The Fabric Project on GitHub, Convergence features a suite of customizable prompts that empower users to personalize their experience and tailor it to their unique needs and workflows.

Features

  • **Offline Access**: Convergence allows users to run LLMs locally on their computer without requiring an internet connection.
  • **LLM Integration**: The app enables seamless integration with Large Language Models (LLMs) for tasks such as text generation, language processing, and more.
  • **Customizable Prompts**: Inspired by The Fabric Project on GitHub, Convergence features a set of customizable prompts that allow users to tailor their experience and adapt it to their specific needs and workflows.
